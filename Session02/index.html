Organization Folders

Perfect for my classroom!

I bought these after seeing Jen Jones (@helloliteracy) post about them holding her Kids A-Z books. They perfectly hold six books and the lesson plan to go with it. Teacher MUST HAVE for organization!

I LOVE these for storing my math centers and small group materials! They are sturdy and have all continued to hold up and work well after frequent use. The little pocket allows for a description of what is inside each envelope. I love them so much I bought a second set just like them!

Durable!

These envelopes are durable and hold up great for centers in kindergarten! They are not slippery like plastic zip bags, so storage is easier and they fit into a file cabinet or storage container with no difficulties. Smaller pieces can easily fit inside along with standard size paper. I also like the pocket on the outside for labeling purposes. I use a color-coding system in my class for subjects, so the multiple colors works well. My only wish is that this product came in singular colors, as I need more of certain colors than others.

PERFECT SIZE for paper scraps - large or small
